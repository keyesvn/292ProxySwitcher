# Privacy Policy for 292ProxySwitcher

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Data Collection
This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
All proxy settings and credentials are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.
No analytics, tracking, or background telemetry is utilized.

## Permissions
- `proxy`: Required to modify browser proxy settings in order to route your traffic through your specified proxies.
- `storage`: Required to save your proxy configuration locally so you don't have to enter it every time.
- `webRequest` & `webRequestAuthProvider`: Required to automatically provide authentication credentials to your proxies when prompted by the browser.
- `<all_urls>` host permission: Required to intercept authentication requests on all websites to inject proxy credentials seamlessly.

## Single Purpose
The single and only purpose of this extension is to manage local proxy settings for the user.

## Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
